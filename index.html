Our Services

The Best Daycare

Little Genesis preschool offers exceptional day care services that prioritize the holistic development and well-being of children. With a warm and nurturing environment, our preschool aims to create a second home where children feel safe, supported, and encouraged to explore their potential.

Safety is a top priority, and the premises is equipped with CCTV surveillance, and child-friendly facilities. Regular communication channels are established with parents to keep them informed about their child's progress, daily activities, and any necessary updates.

Discovery Program 1

This program is designed for children aged 1.6 to 2.3 years. It provides a nurturing and stimulating environment where toddlers explore their surroundings, engage in age-appropriate activities, and develop essential skills through play-based learning.

Discovery Program 2

This program caters to children aged 2.4 to 3 years. This program offers an enriching environment where toddlers engage in interactive play, develop social skills, and explore early literacy and numeracy concepts through hands-on activities and guided exploration.

Nursery

This program welcomes children aged 3 years and older. It is crafted to develop foundational academic skills such as pre-writing skills, life skills and motor skills, foster creativity, and build social connections through play, exploration, and structured learning experiences.

Lower Kindgarten

The program is designed for children aged 4 years and older. This program focuses on developing essential academic and social skills through a well-rounded curriculum that includes interactive learning activities, early literacy and numeracy, creative expression, and collaborative play experiences.

Upper Kindgarten

This program caters to children aged 5 years and older. It provides a structured and supportive learning environment where children build on their foundational skills, develop critical thinking abilities, explore advanced concepts, and prepare for a smooth transition to primary school through engaging and age-appropriate activities.

Social Inclusion Program

This Program is specially designed for children aged 3 to 7 years with development delays and global delays. We offer individualized support, specialized instruction, and therapeutic interventions to promote social integration, enhance communication skills, and foster overall development in a nurturing and inclusive environment.

About us

Our Philosophy

Little Genesis as the name suggests is all about beginnings, experiences, a place where the journey of learning begins. We at Little Genesis are driven by a simple wish to create an environment that is fun, protective and transformative for children. And thus ensuring that in their budding years the child is shaped into a well rounded individual who is primed to embark on a life long journey of learning.

The focus is on promoting the social, emotional, cognitive, and physical growth of children. The preschool values the uniqueness of each child and strives to create an inclusive and supportive community where every child feels valued and respected.

Moreover, the preschool values open communication and collaboration with parents, recognizing them as partners in their child's educational journey.

Our Activities

Outdoor Trip

We believe in the importance of outdoor exploration and hands-on learning. Our outdoor trips provide children with enriching experiences, such as nature walks, field trips to local parks, and interactive outdoor activities.

These excursions foster a deeper connection with nature, stimulate curiosity, and promote physical development in a fun and safe environment.

Sand Play

It is a cherished activity that sparks imagination and learning. With buckets and shovels in hand, children explore the sensory wonders of the sandbox. They build towering castles, dig deep trenches, and experiment with different textures.

Through sand play, they develop fine motor skills, creativity, and social interaction as they collaborate and share ideas.

Story Telling

At Little Genesis, storytelling comes alive with enchantment and wonder. In a cozy corner filled with vibrant books, young minds embark on imaginative journeys. Teachers weave captivating tales, igniting curiosity and fostering a love for literature.

With every story, children's imaginations soar, transporting them to magical realms and teaching valuable life lessons.

Festival Celebration

We host a vibrant array of festival celebrations throughout the year, creating joyous and memorable experiences for the children. From the colorful festivities of Diwali and Holi to the enchanting spirit of Christmas, we embrace diversity and cultural traditions.

These celebrations foster a sense of unity, appreciation, and excitement, making Little Genesis Preschool a place where every child feels connected and cherished.
